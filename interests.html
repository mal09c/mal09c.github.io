Books

I love to read and am a huge fan of American literature. One of my favorite courses in college was on literature during the Great Depression.

Comics

With the rise of comic book movies and having been a fan of graphic novels in high school, I've recently become a regular comic book reader. There's a great variety of characters and stories that push the limit and also offer a wider range of interesting female characters.

Movies and TV

I am an aspiring film buff and I love watching films by people as varied as Ingmar Bergman to John Ford to Kelly Reichardt. Whether a foreign film or the latest superhero blockbuster, I love analyzing how these stories impact the public and what they say about the world.
